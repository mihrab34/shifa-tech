ShiVent

Why?

Shifa Technologies was established in response to the Covid-19 pandemic to address the shortage supply of ventilators which is critical to supporting patiets with respiratory distress.

What?

We have developed ShiVent: a non-electric , non-invasive ventilator that operates on the principle of bubble CPAC (Continous Positive Airways Pressure) that prevents the Airways deterioration.

Learn More

Digital Platform

Digitalization of a non-electrical product is pushing the boundaries of possibilities. Our digital platform offers onboarding, installation, usage training and manuals for Doctors, Nurses and Medical practitioners on how to use the ShiVent and manage patient records. Furthermore, it enables medical practitioners to share experiences while adhering to GDPR standards.

Functionality Test

ShiVent has passed 2 functionality tests carried out on:

1-May 2020 at the National Centre for Sports and Exercise Medicine, Loughborough University, United Kingdom.

2-10th June 2020 at the Royal Infirmary Hospital. Leicester, United Kingdom.

We are planning a pilot project in Lagos, Nigeria for October, 2020.
