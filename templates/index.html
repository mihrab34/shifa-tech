ShiVent

Who We are

Shifa Technologies Ltd was set up in response to the Covid-19 pandemic given the limited supply of ventilators and the strain that it has caused for health systems globally

Product

Bubble CPAP Kit

The ShiVent is a Bubble CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure)kit with specialised cap and an oxygen/air blender offering a non-invasive mode of ventilation to assist COVID19 patients with breathing difficulties.

Low Cost 3D-printed Material

The kit comprises of a mixture of 3D-printed components and readily available materials within hospital settings such as medical tubing, oxygen mask and plastic jar available off the shelf.

Easy Set-up and Non-Electricity Dependent

Low tech, easy to set up and non-electricity dependent.Cost only a fraction of the cost of regular Bubble CPAP which average $2,000.

Low Resource Setting

It is also able to plug into existing oxygen supply channels in hospitals, making it suitable for low resource settings.
