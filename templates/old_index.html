ShiVent

Why?

"Shifa Technologies" was estalished in response the Covid-19 pandemic to address the shortage supply of ventilators which is critical to supporting patients with respiratory distress.

What?

We have developeved Shivent: a non-electric, non-invasive ventilator that operates on the principle of bubble CPAC (Continous Positive Airways Pressure) that prevents the Airways deterioration.

Functionality Test

Shivent has passed 2 functionality test carried out on:

1- 23rd May 2020 at the National Centre for Sports and Exercise Medicine, Loughborough University, United Kingdom

2- 10th June 2020 at the Royal Infirmary Hospital, Leicester, United Kingdom

We are planning a pilot project in Lagos Nigeria for October 2020

Digital Platform

Digitalization of a non-electrical product is pushing the boundaries of possibilities. Our digital platform offers onboarding, installation, usage training and manuals for Doctors, Nurses and Medical practioners on how to use the ShiVent and manage patient records. Futhermore,it enables medical practioners to share experiences while adhereing to GDPR standards
